Hello World

Hello everyone! Welcome to my page. I'm guessing you're reading this to know a bit more about who I am and what I do. I generally dislike talking about myself but I hope the info below will suffice.

I was originally born and raised oceanside, your typical beach bum, in an island just shy off the east coast called, Deerfield Beach, in sunny South Florida. About halfway through my Marketing degree, I took an intro to technology class at my local university and was absolutely fascinated. It was like blood in the water for me, I had just gotten my first taste of the code world... I immediately decided to drop out school and move to Miami, where I'd be attending an academy that taught something called JavaScript -- the rest is history! After about a year of dealing with promises & callbacks, learning some cool computer science theories, and actually having the opportunity to teach a few software concepts of my own, I now consider myself a full-stack JavaScript engineer. I find myself to be overly passionate about the work that I do, and to be honest, I love every second of the research that's done, and every line of code that's typed. I am now tackling the insane field of Artificial Intelligence, focusing mostly on machine learning and computer vision. My life long dream is to one day use the skills I possess in software to make a positive environmental impact in our ecosystems, more specifically: our beaches.
